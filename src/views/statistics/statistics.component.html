Pizza Indicator

The Pizza Indicator tracks the cost of pizza ingredients to show inflation. It calculates the average growth of commodities related to pizza ingredients, such as wheat for flour, livestock for meat, corn for corn syrup, grains for tomatoes, and agriculture for cheese. A rise in the Pizza Indicator represents an increase in the cost of pizza ingredients, indicating inflation in food prices.
